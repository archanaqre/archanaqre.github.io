02 / Research

Selected research.

Papers, workshops, and ongoing work.

  • 2026Under Review

    When Do Agents Go Wrong: A Failure-Centric Evaluation of Agentic Systems

    A failure-centric evaluation of agentic systems — characterising where and how LLM-driven agents fail, with the goal of moving evaluation beyond aggregate success rates.

  • 2025Submitted to ICLR 2026 (Under Review)

    Magentic Marketplace: An Open-Source Environment for Studying Agentic Markets

    An open-source environment for studying agentic markets — a two-sided marketplace with LLM-driven consumer and business agents that lets researchers measure consumer welfare and analyse LLM agent behaviour in controlled economic settings.

    PaperMicrosoft Blog
  • 2025ACL 2025 (Industry Track) — Oral Presentation

    From Recall to Creation: Generating Follow-Up Questions using Bloom's Taxonomy and Grice's Maxims

    A follow-up question generation approach grounded in Bloom's Taxonomy that produces cognitively complex queries for testing conversational AI assistants, together with GriceWise — a set of reference-free evaluation metrics based on Grice's Maxims (Logical Consistency, Informativeness, Relevance, Clarity) that achieve over 0.6 Spearman correlation with human judgments.

    Paper
  • 2024Submitted to ARR August 2024 (Pre-print)

    Whisper-ing with Subtitles: Making TV Shows Chatty for All

    A pipelined approach using Whisper for automatic speech recognition together with audio-event-detection models (EnCLAP and Whisper-AT) to generate automated closed captions for the Deaf and Hard-of-Hearing, reaching a 15% word error rate.
